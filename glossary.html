🏦 Acquiring Bank (Acquirer)

The financial institution (or processor acting on its behalf) that provides the merchant account and ultimately deposits card payments into the merchant's bank account.

Why It Matters When a merchant asks where their money is, the acquiring bank is one of the organizations involved.

Related: Settlement • Funding • Merchant Account
